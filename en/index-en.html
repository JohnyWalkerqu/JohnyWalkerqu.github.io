Prototype Cabin

Our goals with this prototype are:

  • getting experience in the aproach of building camping cabins for european pickup's
  • realization of an old dream and desires
  • testing the feasability and market acceptance
  • first steps to the camping business

2019

start

The initial project idea goes back to the 1970s at the yearly Corsica vacation. Finally in 2019, after visiting the camping and travel fair in Stuttgart CMT and several other camping fairs the idea to built a prototype was born.

1000

screws

The shell of the cube is mounted with dozens of screws. There are two reasons to do it this way. The first one is the unique design we can create from it, while the second gives more flexibilty in future changes of the panels.

20000

hours thought

Many hours of thoughts, drafts and discussion went into the prototype with several people familiar or non-familiar with the matter as well. Those talks formed the ideas and current state of realization.

About us

Who are we?

  • We run a carpentry since the early 1960s when our grand-grand or grand-father started to build furnitures after his master training with a small number of machines on his own.
  • Two generations later the company had grown and started to work on more complex and greater projects on behalf of their clients. As the experience and machine park had grown over time the ever-since dream of building a very own designed, engineered and manufactured product got stronger.
  • During the midtime of 2019 on a melting hot summer vacation day on a camping site on the island of Corsica a strange but also attracting kind of camping vehicle moved along the water front road where it was spotted by the eyes of Michael and Heike Walk.
